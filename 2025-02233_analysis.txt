
Order number                            : Executive Order 14191
Date signed                             : January 29, 2025
Signed by                               : President Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order aims to expand educational freedom and opportunity for families by promoting school choice initiatives. It directs federal agencies to issue guidance and explore mechanisms to support state-based K-12 educational choice programs using existing funding streams. The order emphasizes parental rights in choosing their children's education and encourages the use of discretionary grant programs to foster educational freedom.
