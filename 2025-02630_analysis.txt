
Order number                            : 14204
Date signed                             : February 7, 2025
Signed by                               : The President
Detailed Summary                        : This Executive Order addresses the Republic of South Africa's recent Expropriation Act 13 of 2024, which allows for the seizure of ethnic minority Afrikaners' agricultural property without compensation. The order condemns South Africa's actions as unjust and immoral and outlines measures to halt U.S. aid to South Africa while promoting resettlement assistance for affected Afrikaners.
