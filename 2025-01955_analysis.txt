
Order number                            : 14153
Date signed                             : January 20, 2025
Signed by                               : Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This Executive Order prioritizes the development of Alaska's natural resources (energy, mineral, timber, and seafood). It seeks to reverse what it views as restrictive policies enacted by previous administrations, aiming to maximize resource production on both federal and state lands in Alaska. The order directs various agencies to take specific actions, including rescinding or revising regulations that impede resource development, reinstating previously canceled leases, and streamlining permitting processes.
