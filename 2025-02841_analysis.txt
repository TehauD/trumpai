
Order number                            : Executive Order 14211
Date signed                             : February 12, 2025
Signed by                               : Joe Biden
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order focuses on reforming the U.S. Foreign Service to ensure faithful implementation of the President's foreign policy agenda. It emphasizes the role of the Secretary of State in overseeing and directing foreign policy execution, outlines personnel procedures for addressing performance and conduct issues within the Foreign Service, and mandates reforms in recruiting, performance evaluations, retention standards, and training programs.
