Detailed Summary                        : This Executive Order reinstates the federal death penalty and directs the Attorney General to pursue capital punishment for a wider range of crimes. It criticizes previous administrations for halting executions and commuting sentences, arguing that capital punishment is essential for deterring heinous crimes and upholding justice.
