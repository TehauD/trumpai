
Order number                            : Executive Order 14152
Date signed                             : January 20, 2025
Signed by                               : Joseph R. Biden
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order revokes security clearances of former intelligence officials who engaged in partisan political activity related to the 2020 presidential election. It cites their letter discrediting Hunter Biden's laptop story as an example of misuse of intelligence expertise for political gain. It also targets John Bolton for publishing classified information in his memoir.
