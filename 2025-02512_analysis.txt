
Order number                            : 14200
Date signed                             : February 5, 2025
Signed by                               : The President
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order amends a previous order (issued February 1, 2025) that imposed duties to address the synthetic opioid supply chain in the People's Republic of China. The amendment specifically changes subsection (g) of section 2, relating to duty-free de minimis treatment for covered articles.
