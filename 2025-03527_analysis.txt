
Order number                            : 14222
Date signed                             : February 26, 2025
Signed by                               : The President
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order aims to increase transparency and accountability in Federal spending on contracts, grants, and loans. It requires agencies to establish systems for recording payments, justifying expenditures, reviewing existing contracts and grants, and streamlining contracting processes. The order also imposes a temporary freeze on non-essential travel funded by the federal government and mandates a review of federally owned real property.
