
Order number                            : 14177
Date signed                             : January 23, 2025
Signed by                               : President
Detailed Summary                        : This Executive Order establishes the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST) to advise the President on matters involving science, technology, education, and innovation policy. The council will provide scientific and technical information needed to inform public policy relating to various topics, including the American economy, workforce, national security, and more. It replaces a previous council established by Executive Order 14007.
