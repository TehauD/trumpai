
Order number                            : 14167
Date signed                             : January 20, 2025
Signed by                               : Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order directs the U.S. military to play a more active role in securing the nation's borders, particularly the southern border. It emphasizes the protection of territorial integrity and sovereignty against threats such as unlawful migration, drug trafficking, and transnational crime. The order mandates revisions to the Unified Command Plan to give USNORTHCOM the mission of sealing the borders and maintaining security.
