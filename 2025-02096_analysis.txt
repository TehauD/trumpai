
Order number                            : 14172
Date signed                             : January 20, 2025
Signed by                               : Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order aims to restore the names of American landmarks and geographic features to honor individuals considered American heroes. It specifically reinstates the name "Mount McKinley" and renames the "Gulf of Mexico" to the "Gulf of America". The order also directs the review and potential replacement of members on the U.S. Board on Geographic Names and encourages the consideration of additional patriotic figures for future naming honors.
