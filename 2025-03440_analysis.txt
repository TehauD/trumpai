
Order number                            : Executive Order 14221
Date signed                             : February 25, 2025
Signed by                               : Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This Executive Order aims to increase price transparency in the healthcare system. It builds upon previous efforts during the Trump administration's first term and seeks to ensure patients have access to clear and accurate pricing information for medical services and prescription drugs. The order directs federal agencies to enforce existing price transparency regulations, standardize pricing data, and take measures to ensure compliance.
