
Order number                            : 14215
Date signed                             : February 18, 2025
Signed by                               : The President
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order aims to increase presidential supervision and control over independent regulatory agencies. It mandates review of proposed regulations by the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs (OIRA), establishes performance standards for agency heads, allows adjustments to agency budgets based on presidential priorities, and requires regular consultation between agencies and the White House.
