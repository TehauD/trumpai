
Order number                            : Executive Order 14207
Date signed                             : February 10, 2025
Signed by                               : Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order eliminates the Federal Executive Institute, a program providing leadership training to bureaucrats. It argues that the institute has contributed to an enlarged and entrenched Washington bureaucracy that does not benefit the American family. The order revokes previous documents establishing or requiring the existence of the institute.
