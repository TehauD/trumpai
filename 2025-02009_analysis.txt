
Order number                            : 14161
Date signed                             : January 20, 2025
Signed by                               : The President (Donald Trump)
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order focuses on enhancing vetting and screening of aliens seeking admission to the United States. It aims to protect national security and public safety by identifying potential threats and ensuring that admitted aliens do not harbor hostile attitudes towards the U.S., its citizens, culture, government or institutions.
                                          
                                          The order directs the Secretary of State, in coordination with other agencies, to identify resources for enhanced vetting, determine information needs from foreign countries for visa applications, re-establish a uniform baseline for screening and vetting standards, and vet all aliens seeking admission, particularly those from high-risk regions.
                                          
                                          A report is required within 60 days identifying countries with deficient vetting information that may warrant suspension of admissions. The order also mandates the evaluation and adjustment of regulations related to grounds of inadmissibility, safeguarding against inadequate refugee identification verification, and reviewing visa programs for potential misuse by hostile actors.
                                          
                                          Furthermore, it recommends actions to protect Americans from foreign nationals undermining fundamental rights, ensuring adequate resources for offenses described in 8 U.S.C. 1451, evaluating assimilation programs, and recommending additional measures to protect the nation from threats.
