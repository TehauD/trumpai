
Order number                            : 14209
Date signed                             : February 10, 2025
Signed by                               : President Donald Trump
Detailed Summary                        : This executive order pauses enforcement of the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) for a period of 180 days to allow for a review of its application and impact on U.S. foreign policy and economic competitiveness. The order argues that the FCPA has been overextended beyond its original intent, harming American businesses abroad and impeding the President's Article II authority over foreign affairs.
